With over 10 years of experience in software engineering, I have built impactful applications and tools that address both technical challenges and user needs. My journey began with a strong foundation in IT and business, which I complemented with an Honours BSc and a Software Design and Development course. This background, along with 7 years in Application Support, gave me a unique perspective on the user experience, which has been key in my approach to designing and developing software.

My career took off at IBM, where I worked on innovative projects using emerging technologies like Dojo and JavaScript, building features for the IBM Verse webmail product and leading automation efforts with Jasmine and Selenium. I was also deeply involved in accessibility, ensuring features met the highest standards.

At Bazaarvoice, I transitioned to full-stack development, where I’ve led complex technical projects using modern frameworks like React and Node.js, and built solutions on AWS infrastructure. I've driven initiatives that enhanced privacy and security, improved data authentication processes, and contributed to building a positive, inclusive company culture by mentoring teams and leading onboarding programs.

I am passionate about continuous learning, and my journey has always been about embracing new challenges—whether it’s integrating new APIs, adopting new programming languages, or innovating with cloud technologies. My approach to engineering balances technical expertise with a focus on collaboration, mentorship, and improving team performance.
